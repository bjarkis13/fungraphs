Data Acquisition and Visualisation, T-416-VIFG

The Icelandic Regional Development Institute defines municipalities which have 15 percent or more depopulation over the last 15 years as municipalities with persistent depopulation. We want to examine these municipalities on how they differ from others in population, expenses and age distribution.
